FAQ

Common Questions

Does the AI understand Bangla?
Yes — our AI is trained on both Bangla and English and handles mixed-language messages (Banglish) naturally. This is critical for Bangladeshi e-commerce, and we've optimised specifically for it.
What happens when the AI doesn't understand?
The AI gracefully asks for clarification. If it still cannot help after 2 attempts, it sends a polite message and alerts you (admin) on WhatsApp or Telegram so a human can step in — with the full conversation context.
Can I customise what the AI says?
Absolutely. We build the entire conversation flow around your brand's tone and voice. You can approve every response template before launch, and we can update them at any time after go-live.
How long does setup take?
A standard AI order-taking system takes 5–7 days from discovery call to live deployment. Complex setups with multiple platforms and custom databases may take up to 14 days.
Is there ongoing support after launch?
Yes. Every project includes 2 weeks of post-launch monitoring and support. After that, we offer monthly maintenance retainers to update flows, add new products and fine-tune performance.
