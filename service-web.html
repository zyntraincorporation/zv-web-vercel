FAQ

Questions Answered

How long does a website take to build?
Landing pages: 3–4 days. Business sites: 7–10 days. E-commerce: 10–14 days. These timelines assume you provide all content (text, images, logo) upfront.
Do I own the website files after delivery?
Yes, 100%. After final payment you receive all source files, assets and full ownership. You can host it anywhere and modify it however you like — no lock-in.
Will the site work on all devices?
Absolutely. We test on 15+ device/browser combinations — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge on desktop, tablet, and 4 mobile screen sizes — before handover. Mobile-first is not optional for us, it's the default.
Can I update the site myself after launch?
Yes. For simple sites we provide a video walkthrough explaining how to edit content. For larger projects we can add a CMS (WordPress or custom admin panel) so you can update text, images and products without touching code.
