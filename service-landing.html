01Hero Section
The most critical 3 seconds — a powerful headline that speaks directly to the customer's pain, a supporting subheadline and one crystal-clear CTA. We A/B test headline variants before finalising.
+28% impact on bounce rate
02Social Proof
Real customer reviews, order counts and trust badges placed strategically above the fold. Social proof removes the biggest barrier to purchase — uncertainty.
+35% trust signals increase CVR
03Product / Offer Block
High-quality product presentation with benefit-first copywriting. We highlight what the customer gains, not just what the product is. Images, pricing and value proposition in one tight block.
Clarity = 40% higher intent
04Benefit Bullets
3–6 benefit points written in "you" language. Not features — benefits. We use the FAB (Feature → Advantage → Benefit) framework to make every bullet count.
Scannable content reduces exits
05Urgency & Scarcity
Ethical urgency — limited stock counters, countdown timers or limited-time pricing. When used correctly this is one of the highest-impact conversion elements on a landing page.
+22% increase in same-day orders
06CTA / Order Form
The form is simple — minimum fields, maximum clarity. One action, zero distractions. Button copy is action-oriented ("Order Now" not "Submit"). Form is connected to your WhatsApp or database automatically.
Simple forms convert 3x better

FAQ

Common Questions

What's the difference between a landing page and a website?
A landing page has one single goal — convert a visitor into a buyer or lead. It has no navigation menu or external links to distract the visitor. A website has many goals and many pages. Landing pages always outperform full websites when you're running paid ads.
How do I receive orders from the landing page?
We connect your order form to Google Sheets (for organised tracking), send you a WhatsApp notification for each new order, and optionally trigger an automated confirmation message to the customer. You always know the moment someone orders.
Do you write the copy (text) for the page?
Yes. We write all copy using conversion copywriting principles — benefit-focused headlines, FAB framework bullets and action CTAs. We just need a product brief from you. If you prefer to write your own copy, we can work with that too.
Can I use the same landing page for multiple ad sets?
Absolutely. One landing page can serve multiple ad sets — in fact we recommend this to keep your message consistent. For significantly different audiences we recommend creating 2 variants, which we can build for a discounted rate.
